About Jibhi

Jibhi, nestled in the lap of the majestic Jalori Pass, is a hidden gem of the Himachal Pradesh region. The town is surrounded by lush green cedar trees that provide a serene and peaceful environment to its visitors. The melodious chirping of birds and the refreshing fragrance of pine trees will enchant you throughout your stay.

Jibhi's picturesque surroundings make it an ideal destination for trekking and hiking. The region is dotted with numerous trails that will take you through the dense forest, and the spectacular views of the surrounding mountain ranges will leave you spellbound.

The town is also known for its vibrant local culture and traditions. You can immerse yourself in the local customs and rituals, taste the local cuisine, and shop for traditional handicrafts and souvenirs.
